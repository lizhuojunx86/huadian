## 1. 信件 C — 会计 / 审计 / 舞弊检测学者（英文 / 对口 08）

**找人配方（10 分钟）**：
1. 打开 SSRN（ssrn.com）搜索：`earnings management detection` 或 `benchmark beating`，按日期排序，看近 2 年有稿的作者（邮箱在论文首页）。
2. 备选：Google Scholar 搜 `Burgstahler Dichev 1997` → 点"被引用次数" → 筛近 2 年 → 挑 2-3 位仍活跃的作者。
3. 选人标准：近 2 年在这个题目上有产出（回复率最高）；不必是大牛，助理教授/博后更可能回信。

**Subject:** A GMP analogue of benchmark-beating — detecting reverse-calculated batch records

> Dear Dr. 【对方姓氏】,
>
> I'm an independent researcher working on data integrity in pharmaceutical (Traditional Chinese Medicine) manufacturing. I've just posted a working paper on SSRN [https://ssrn.com/abstract=7099138] that documents what appears to be a manufacturing analogue of earnings benchmark-beating: batch yields across six batches with σ ≈ 0.02 percentage points, hugging the spec lower bound — operators "manage the process, not the books," which echoes the Graham/Harvey/Rajgopal finding that executives prefer real actions over accounting adjustments.
>
> Your work on 【对方论文】 is directly related to the threshold-clustering angle. I'd be grateful for a brief reaction to one question: **in your view, does the single-enterprise (n=2 products) boundary undercut the core claim, or is the interview-confirmed mechanism enough to justify the detection framework as a hypothesis worth testing?**
>
> No obligation to read the full paper — a one-paragraph reaction would already help a lot. The de-identified data are public (CC BY 4.0) if useful.
>
> Best regards,
> [你的署名] (Independent Researcher)

## 2. 信件 B — STS / 组织社会学学者（英文 / 对口 09 的理论框架，锚 08 预印）

**找人配方（10 分钟）**：
1. Google Scholar 搜 `compliance decoupling audit` 或 `Scott seeing like a state metis` → 点被引 → 筛近 2 年。
2. 备选：搜 `inscription Latour laboratory records` 的近期引用者；或 ResearchGate 站内搜 `GMP ethnography` / `paperwork compliance`。
3. 选人标准：做"合规文书 vs 实际实践"经验研究的人（民族志/组织研究背景优先）。ResearchGate 可站内私信，门槛低于冷邮件。

**Subject:** Batch records as inscriptions — a three-layer model of GMP compliance narratives

> Dear Dr. 【对方姓氏】,
>
> I'm an independent researcher studying pharmaceutical batch records. A working paper I've just posted [https://ssrn.com/abstract=7099138] argues that GMP batch records are **layered artifacts**: a compliance-narrative surface (largely reverse-calculated), an unwritten middle layer of actual operational decisions, and a deep layer of embodied operator strategy. The framing draws on Latour's inscriptions, Scott's legibility/métis distinction, and communities-of-practice theory — operators' edge-hugging turns out to be a rational, apprenticeship-transmitted strategy, not an individual moral failure.
>
> Given your work on 【对方论文】, I'd value your reaction to one question: **is the three-layer split operationalizable as a field-research category, or does it collapse in practice into the familiar formal/informal dichotomy?**
>
> A one-paragraph reaction would already be very helpful. (A companion paper focused on the layered-narrative structure is in the public project repository.)
>
> Best regards,
> [你的署名] (Independent Researcher)
> lizhuojun@gmail.com

## 3. 信件 A — 制药 QA / 数据完整性实务专家（中文 / 对口 08+10）

**找人配方（10 分钟）**：
1. **蒲公英论坛**：你发帖后，看"数据完整性"版块里高质量回帖者/版主，站内私信（最自然，有帖子做上下文）。
2. 备选：知乎搜"数据完整性 GMP"高赞回答者；微信公众号"蒲公英""GMP办公室"等文章作者（文末常有联系方式）。
3. 选人标准：写过数据完整性实操内容的人（药企 QA、第三方审计、前核查员转咨询均可）。

**Subject（或私信首句）：** 请教一个数据完整性自查方法在实务中是否成立

> 【对方姓氏】老师您好，
>
> 冒昧打扰。我是独立研究者，之前在中药企业做过管理，最近把"批记录反算填表的统计检测"整理成了一篇方法稿（英文预印版：https://ssrn.com/abstract=7099138；中文简版我发在蒲公英论坛，标题是《来料检验/批记录里的"反算填表"，能不能用分布检测自查出来？》）。
>
> 核心思路两条：① 反算有方向性——来料含量偏"往上写"、收率偏"压线写"，两种机制并存；② 检测必须按指标量级分通道，混在一起算会把信号稀释掉。
>
> 想请教您一个实务问题：**"主成分含量贴线 + 跨批 σ 趋近 0"这种形态，在您经手的真实企业数据里常见吗？现行自查/核查会把它当异常线索吗？** 您一句话的判断对我校准这套方法非常有价值。
>
> 顺颂业安
